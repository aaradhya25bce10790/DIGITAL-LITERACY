Do's

1.USE PRIVACY SETTINGS:REGULARLY REVIEW WHO CAN SEE YOUR POSTS AND PERSONAL INFORMATION ON PLATFORMS LIKE INSTAGRAM AND FACEBOOK.

2.FACT-CHECK BEFORE SHARING: VERIFY THE CREDIBILITY OF NEWS OR CLAIMS USING LATERAL READING BEFORE HITTING THE SHARE BUTTON.

3.NETWORK PROFESSIONALLY: USE PLATFORMS LIKE LINKEDIN TO FOLLOW INDUSTRY LEADERS AND JOIN GROUPS RELATED TO YOUR MAJOR.

4.SHOWCASE YOUR PROJECTS: SHARE LINKS TO YOUR GITHUB OR KAGGLE ACHIEVEMENTS TO BUILD A POSITIVE"DIGITAL BRAND".

5.PRACTICE "ETIQUETTE": BE RESPECTFUL IN COMMENTS AND DIRECT MESSAGES,TREATING PEOPLE ONLINE AS YOU WOULD IN PERSON.


DONT's

1.POST IMPLUSIVELY:AVOID POSTING WHEN YOU ARE ANGRY,UPSET OR IN A HIGHLY EMOTIONAL STATE;"THINK BEFORE YOU CLICK."

2.SHARE SENSITIVE INFO: NEVER POST YOUR HOME ADDRESS,PHONE NUMBER,OR CURRENT REAL-TIME LOCATION(WAIT UNTIL YOU HAVE LEFT).

3.ENGAGE WITH TROLLS: DONT WASTE TIME ARGUING WITH ANONYMOUS ACCOUNTS OR ENGAGING IN "FLAME WARS" THAT CAN TURN TOXIC.

4.PLAGIARIZE CONTENT: NEVER POST SOMEONE ELSES WORK,ART OR RESEARCH WITHOUT PERMISSION.

5.IGNORE YOUR DIGITAL FOOTPRINT: DONT ASSUME DELETED POSTS ARE GONE OREVER;SOMEONE MAY HAVE SCREENSHOTTED OR ARCHIVED THEM.
